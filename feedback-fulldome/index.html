Feedback Fulldome, an anti-futurist musical

2025

  • Fulldome film
  • musical
  • 20 minutes

An experience by Collective AMiXR.
Directed by Claudix Vanesix.
Produced by Lorena García – AMiXR, in co-production with WeMakeVR (Netherlands).
Sound Direction and Music by Cocompi.

After ingesting a mystical beverage, the user will be drawn into a spiritual ritual that takes the form of a digital nightmare. In this liminal space, their physical bodies and ancestral traditions confront the infinite possibilities and threats of the virtual world.
