The Bastard Images

2024

  • documentary
  • 62 minutes

Directed by Marianela Vega Oroza.
Produced by Lali Madueño Medina & Blue Producciones.
Sound design by Mirella Bellido C. and Cocompi.
Music composed by Cocompi – Adoro el Sonidito Records.

Embracing a temporary visual impairment, a filmmaker immerses in her family's 1990s home movies, composing a narrative that reimagines her father's vision.
They reconnect in the present, amid vulnerability, awkwardness and love: the story of a gardener and a one-eyed woman.
