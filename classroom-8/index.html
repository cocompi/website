Classroom 8

2023

  • documentary
  • 54 minutes

Directed by Héctor Gálvez.
Produced by Héctor Gálvez – Piedra Alada.
Music composed by Cocompi – Adoro el Sonidito Records.

From his modest apartment in Lima, a teacher gives virtual classes, seeking to reflect with his young students with low socio-economic backgrounds on racism, politics and inequality: issues that resonate in an increasingly fractured country.

Nominated for Best Documentary Award 2023 – Peruvian Film Press Association APRECI

Official Selection in Festival de Cine de Lima PUCP
Category: documentary (Peru - 2023)

Official Selection in Semana del Cine ULima
Category: feature film (Peru - 2023)
