Ephemeral, with Jorge Corpuna

2020

  • documentary
  • experimental
  • 5 minutes

Co-directed by Ana Lía Accinelli and Cocompi.
Sound Design and Music by Cocompi.

A collaborative, online/onsite hybrid exploration inspired by the work of Jorge Corpuna, visual artist from Cusco, Peru. 

We get an insight not only from his watercolor pieces, but also his influences and homeland, inviting us into his thoughts, emotions and feelings.
