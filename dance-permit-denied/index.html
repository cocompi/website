An experience by Collective AMiXR.
Directed by Claudix Vanesix.
Produced by Lorena García – AMiXR.
Sound Direction and Music by Cocompi.

Claudix Vanesix was born female and now lives as a non-binary trans person.
In this fusion of theatrical dance performance and 360 documentary, they confront the gender-based exclusion in the traditional Peruvian dance, that is passed down in their family from father to son. They challenge the exclusive rights of men in Peruvian society by performing the dance naked, asserting their place and identity in their culture.
