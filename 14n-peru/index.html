14N – PERU

2020

  • documantary
  • archive, experimental
  • 5 minutes

Direction, Sound Design, and Music by Cocompi.

Reconstruction of events surrounding the massive protests after the legislative coup that made Manuel Merino president of Peru in november 2020. 

Making exclusive use of media recorded by citizens at home and at streets, we portray circumstances, responses, consequences and a litte beam of collective memory to –hopefully– never forget.

Comissioned for Exhibition “Rhythmic Alchemy”
from Sound Art Gallery “Ruido/Noise” (USA – 2020)
